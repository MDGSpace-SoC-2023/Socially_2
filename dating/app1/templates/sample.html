Initial Selection Process:

  • Users register and select three people from the crowd.
  • The system ensures mutual selections for a more comfortable interaction.
  • If Person A selects Person B and vice versa, their profiles are shared in each other's inboxes.

Inbox Functionality:

  • Users' inboxes contain profiles of individuals they have mutually selected.
  • This approach helps initiate conversations between people who have already shown interest in each other.

Profile Sorting:

  • Users can view and sort profiles based on three parameters:
    • Long Distance: Sorting profiles based on proximity or distance.
    • Taken: Identifying individuals who are already in relationships.
    • Single: Identifying individuals who are currently single.

Preferences Customization:

  • Users have the option to edit their preferences based on personal criteria.
  • This customization allows individuals to tailor their search and match preferences.
